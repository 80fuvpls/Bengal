I am a 12th-grade student at Gia Dinh High School, with a deep interest in photography in particular and the arts in general. I use photography as a way to express emotions that have long been held within me; at the same time, it reflects how I perceive and respond to issues in society.


To me, every photograph carries a message. Through my work, I hope people can sense the range of emotions I seek to convey—feelings of isolation, passion, devotion, and even pain—but above all, a sense of freedom.


I have taken on various roles across different photography projects and organizations; however, my primary roles are creative director and lead photographer.


There is still much about photography—and about the world—that I have yet to fully explore. Nevertheless, I am determined to pursue freedom, so that I may discover it for myself and, in turn, share that sense of freedom with others.
